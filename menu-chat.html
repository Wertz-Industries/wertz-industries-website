Frequently Asked Questions

How does Menu Chat learn about my business?

During setup, you provide your website URL, service list, menu, FAQs, hours, and any other info you want the chat to know. The AI ingests it all and uses it to answer customer questions accurately. You can update the knowledge base anytime.

Do I need a developer to install it?

No. Menu Chat is a single line of code you paste into your website. If you use WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, or similar platforms, we provide one-click install guides. Setup takes about 5 minutes.

What happens when the AI can't answer a question?

The chat captures the customer's name, phone number, and question, then notifies you via SMS or email for a personal follow-up. The customer sees a message like "Great question! Let me have someone get back to you shortly." Nothing falls through the cracks.

Can I customize how the chat looks?

Yes. You can customize the chat bubble color, position, welcome message, and avatar to match your brand. The widget is designed to look native to your website, not like an afterthought.

Does it capture leads when I'm closed?

Absolutely. That's one of its primary functions. When someone visits your site at 11 PM and asks about pricing or availability, Menu Chat answers what it can and captures their contact info so you can follow up in the morning.

Will it make things up or give wrong answers?

Menu Chat only answers based on the information you provide. If it doesn't know something, it says so and offers to connect the customer with your team. It doesn't hallucinate or invent facts about your business.

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. Every plan starts with a 7-day free trial so you can see how it works with real visitors before you pay.
