Frequently Asked Questions

What channels does Support Macros work with?

Support Macros works across email, SMS, and live chat. You write your templates once and use them everywhere. The AI adapts tone and length to fit the channel automatically.

How does the AI suggestion feature work?

When a customer message comes in, Support Macros reads the context and suggests the best matching template from your library. You can insert it with one click, edit it, or ignore the suggestion. It learns from your team's choices over time.

Can different team members have different templates?

Yes. You get a shared team library plus personal templates for each team member. Admins can lock certain templates to ensure consistency, while team members can create their own shortcuts for common situations.

Do templates support personalization?

Absolutely. Templates use variables like customer name, appointment date, service type, and more. When you insert a template, the variables auto-fill from the conversation context. You can also add custom variables specific to your business.

Is there a limit to how many templates I can create?

The Starter plan includes up to 50 templates. Professional includes 200, and Team includes unlimited. Most small businesses find that 30-40 well-crafted templates cover 90% of their conversations.

Does it work with my existing tools?

Support Macros integrates with popular email clients, SMS platforms, and live chat tools. If you're using something custom, our API lets you connect it. Setup typically takes 10-15 minutes per channel.

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. Every plan starts with a 7-day free trial so your team can build templates and see the time savings before you pay anything.
