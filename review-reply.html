Frequently Asked Questions

How does it match my brand voice?

During setup, we analyze your existing review responses and ask about your preferred tone (professional, friendly, casual, or custom). The AI uses this profile to generate replies that sound like you wrote them. You approve the first batch to fine-tune, and the system learns from your edits.

What platforms does it support?

Google Business Profile, Yelp, and Facebook are supported at launch. Additional platforms are added based on customer demand. The Professional and Multi-Location plans include all platforms.

Can I review responses before they are published?

Absolutely. You set the rules: auto-publish 4-5 star reviews, hold 1-3 star reviews for your personal approval, or review everything before it goes live. You are always in control.

What happens when I get a negative review?

Negative reviews (1-2 stars) trigger an instant alert via SMS and email. The system drafts a professional, empathetic response for your review, but never auto-publishes negative review replies without your approval.

Does it work for multi-location businesses?

Yes. Each location gets its own monitoring, brand voice profile, and dashboard. You can manage all locations from a single login or assign location managers with their own access.

Will customers know the reply is AI-generated?

No. The replies are personalized, reference specific details from the review, and match your brand voice. They read like a thoughtful response from the owner -- because that's exactly what they're trained to sound like.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts. Pay month-to-month and cancel anytime. Every plan starts with a free 14-day trial so you can see the quality of the AI-generated responses before committing.
