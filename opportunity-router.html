HVAC Safety Triage: Protect Your Customers

When a no-cooling call comes in, Opportunity Router asks four targeted safety questions and automatically classifies urgency. Critical situations get escalated in seconds.

Critical

Immediate Escalation

Confusion, fainting, or severe symptoms reported. Very high indoor temp with vulnerable person present.

→ Emergency guidance sent + dispatcher alerted within seconds
Urgent

Same-Day Priority

AC completely out with a vulnerable person (elderly, infant) or high indoor temperature reported.

→ Prioritized for same-day dispatch
Standard

Normal Scheduling

No strong risk signals detected. Routine service request captured with full details.

→ Enters normal scheduling queue
This system provides dispatch prioritization and general safety guidance only. It is not medical advice. If you believe there is a medical emergency, contact emergency services immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can Opportunity Router be set up?

Same-day deployment. We handle the remote install -- configure your phone lines, SMS flow, and admin dashboard in a single session. Most businesses are live within a few hours.

Does it work with my existing phone system?

Yes. Opportunity Router works alongside your current phone system. We add a missed-call detection layer via Twilio -- your existing lines, voicemail, and routing stay exactly as they are.

What happens when a customer responds to the SMS?

The system guides them through a short, friendly text conversation: what service they need, their address, whether they're a new or existing customer, and when they'd like a callback. The whole flow takes about 2 minutes.

What is the HVAC Safety Triage feature?

For HVAC companies, the system asks four additional safety questions when a no-cooling call is detected. It classifies calls as CRITICAL, URGENT, or STANDARD and immediately escalates critical situations to your dispatcher. This is dispatch prioritization only -- not medical advice.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts required. Pay month-to-month. If you commit annually, we waive the one-time setup fee. Every plan starts with a free pilot period so you can see results before you pay.

What industries does it work for?

Opportunity Router is built for home service companies: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, solar, landscaping, and general contracting. If your business runs on inbound calls, it works for you.

What if a customer stops responding mid-conversation?

The system detects stale conversations within 5 minutes and notifies your dispatcher with whatever information was captured so far. Nothing is lost -- even a partial lead (phone number + service type) is valuable.
